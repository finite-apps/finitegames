Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2024

Finite Games ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional and rewarded experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: We use Firebase Authentication. If you sign in via Google, we receive your email address and name. For anonymous accounts, we use a unique identifier to save your progress.
  • Device Identifiers: We collect the Android Advertising ID (AAID) and Google Play Services ID to manage rewards and analytics.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions within the App, such as games played and coins earned, via Firebase Analytics.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates third-party SDKs that collect data to perform their functions:

  • Google Firebase: Used for database management (Firestore), authentication, and crash reporting (Crashlytics).
  • Tapjoy: Our rewarded offerwall partner. Tapjoy may collect your device’s advertising ID, IP address, and technical device info to provide and validate coin rewards.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To maintain your coin balance and game unlocks.
  • To facilitate the "Earn More Coins" features via Tapjoy.
  • To analyze app stability and improve game performance.
  • To prevent fraud and duplicate reward claims.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your progress data as long as you have an active account. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address provided below. We will complete the deletion process within 30 days.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the data immediately.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and Firebase Security Rules to ensure that your coins and user data are protected from unauthorized access.
