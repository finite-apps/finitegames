Data Deletion Request

Finite Games respects your privacy. Our app uses anonymous authentication, meaning we do not store your name or email.

How to delete your data:

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Delete My Data. This will instantly wipe your progress and records from our database.
  • By Email: If you cannot access the app, email us at appsfinite at gmail dot com with your internal User ID (found in Settings) to request manual deletion.
